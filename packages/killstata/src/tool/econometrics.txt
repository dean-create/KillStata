The Econometrics tool is an internal compatibility dispatcher for historical workflow replay.

It is not model-visible and must not be presented to users as a general-purpose estimator. New model calls use dedicated, strongly typed tools such as `econometrics_recommend`, `ols_regression`, `panel_fe_regression`, and `iv_2sls`.

Compatibility rules:
- Preserve the estimator recorded by the historical workflow.
- Never infer an identification strategy from a column name.
- Never switch to another estimator automatically.
- Treat missing variables, invalid panel keys, and failed identification requirements as blocking errors.
- Read canonical data through datasetId/stageId whenever available.
- Ground every reported statistic in structured result artifacts from the completed run.
